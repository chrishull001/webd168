About me

I am old enough to remember having a boom box and sitting in my room for hours to make a mixtape of my favorite songs from the radio.

I would record them onto a cassette tape and try to time it just right to avoid the dj rolling in and out. These days I have more songs than I could ever listen to at the touch of my finger, and yet that I always seem to find my way back to my early teen years. It is amazong the emotions that are invoked and the memories that come back from a song.

I also remember having to go wait in line for concert tickets. We would camp out at the record store or the venue to make sure we got a spot.
