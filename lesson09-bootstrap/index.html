Biography for webd168

Hello, my name is Chris Hull. I am a working professional and a father of two daughters. They are the greatest joys of my life. I have an amazing partner who has three children of her own. They have all reached young adulthood which has brought us to a point in our lives where we can do more things we 'always wanted to'. And we are making the most of it!

I am taking this course for a few reasons. Mostly for personal growth. I have been telling myself for many years that I would like to go back to school, and now I have finally taken the first step. This class is me dipping my toes back into the water so to speak. While I have not figured out everything that I want to take or the pace I can go at, I am excited about the opportunities in front of me. Just logging into my own Canvas account is pretty cool, I hope I do not need to ask my children for help.

In my professional life I work for a medical technology company. I work with cloud technologies, primarily AWS and Azure. I have experience with Microsoft's dotnet framework, primarily using c#. While I am regularly around web developers it is not part of my daily routine.

Finally, I am a history buff, I really enjoy reading the stories about the people and places that lie just under the headlines. I have always been fascinated by the British code-breakers of the Second World War. Two summers ago I made it to Bletchley Park, it was amazing to stand in the middle of a place I had read so much about and played such a pivotal role in history.
